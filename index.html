IMPORTANCE OF ASSOCIATION OF A DEVOTEE

My Dear Friends, Here I am going to mention the MOST IMP THING to get Krsna's mercy

It is completely IMPOSSIBLE to get the mercy of the Lord without the mercy of a devotee. One must associate with the devotees to get the platform of devotional service. Even if you associate for a single moment, then you will attain all kinds of success. Also, in history, no one has become a devotee without associating with a Pure Devotee. So, one must render services to the devotees who are regularly engaged in Krsna's service

The Association of a devotee is so auspicious that it can't be compared to being transferred to heaven also. It is because Krsna sends his messenger(devotee) to a person whom he wants to approach him. So, if a person is in association with a devotee then it means that Krsna wants to take that person to his Supreme Abode Golok Dham which is permanent and after attaining that, no one returns to this Sad Material World.

DON'T BE TOO CLOSE WITH THE NON-DEVOTEES

It is the STRICT INSTRUCTION of Chaitanya Mahaprabhu(Incarnation Of Radha & Krsna) that a person who wants spiritual advancement SHOULD NOT ASSOCIATE WITH NON-DEVOTEES of Krsna. Even if you associate with the Non-Devotees, don't share your hearty feelings with them since they may deviate you from the path of Krsna Consciousness. However, while associating with non-devotees, it is important to maintain a respectful and compassionate attitude. By seeing all living beings as part and parcel of the Supreme Lord, we can develop a sense of empathy towards all.

Never disrespect a devotee of Krsna

It is a great offense if a person disrespects a devotee. Krsna may forgive that person who disrespects him but he will never forgive anyone who disrespects his devotee. Anyone who commits such actions is thrown to hell. A devotee can protect a sinful person from Krsna. E.g. Haridas Thakur saved the servants of the Mughal King who had beaten him severely, from Krsna. This is the mercy of a devotee, from this incident, it can be concluded that "The Pure Devotees are more merciful than Krsna himself"

Bhakti of Krsna can't be attained by doing pious activities(Punya karm), by keeping Vrat(Fasting),by opening schools & hospitals or by donating in charity(Dan). One can understand Krsna ONLY by the grace of a devotee. So, to get the mercy of Krsna, one must approach a Pure Devotee. There is NO ALTERNATE WAY!!

Starting Point Of Distress and Darkness In One's Life

The phase of darkness and distress starts in one's life when one is separated from the devotee of Krsna. The association of a devotee is VERY RARE! So, you should try your best to get the Devotee Association. Do listen to the words of His Divine Grace Gopal Krishna Maharaj
